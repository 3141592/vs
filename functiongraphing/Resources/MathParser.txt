-----------------------------------------------------------------
Lundin Reference:
-----------------------------------------------------------------

The parser handles most mathematical operators and functions.

Operators:

+, -, *, /, ^, %


Functions:

sqrt, sin, cos, tan, atan, acos, asin, 
acotan, exp, ln, 10log, fac, sinh, cosh,
tanh, abs, ceil, floor, sfac, round, fpart

Logical:

!, ==, !=, ||, &&, >, < , >=, <=,
